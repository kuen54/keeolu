Fang Yuan

Duty:

Typography

Adobe Illustrator

Personal

2024

Concept

"Fang Yuan" (Square & Circle) is a type experiment exploring the relationship between the golden ratio and typography. In this project, I set a rule: only golden ratio-based perfect circles can be used to carve into the letterforms. The thickness, length, and structure of the characters remain flexible, allowing for future transformation and variation. To ensure legibility, I chose basic Arabic numerals as the foundation of this experiment.

Genetic Tarot

Duty:

Illustration
Creative Strategy
Branding

Adobe Illustrator

Blender+Cycles

Proposal

2020

Insight

As a DNA testing service firm, 23Mofang, like its counterparts, faces the biggest marketing problem - consumers greatly doubt the accuracy of its test results. Paradoxically, as the number of participants increases, the accuracy of the test will continue to rise.

Ideation

Why is it that entertainment like tarot cards, which is free of scientific evidence, is more popular? To eliminate prejudice of consumers, I provided 23Mofang with a visual marketing strategy featuring Tarot and Shadow Puppets, an art form with authentic Chinese DNA.
